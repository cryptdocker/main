One of the biggest risks in crypto isn't volatility — it's connecting to the wrong platform. Phishing sites, rug pulls, and malicious smart contracts cost users billions every year. That's why we built **AI-Powered Risk Analysis** directly into CryptDocker.

## How It Works

When you add a new app or visit any site inside CryptDocker, you can instantly trigger a risk analysis. Our AI engine evaluates the site across multiple dimensions:

- **Domain reputation** — Is this a known, trusted domain? How old is it? Are there any flagged DNS records?
- **Smart contract audit status** — For DeFi protocols, we check whether their contracts have been audited by reputable firms.
- **Community signals** — We aggregate data from social media, forums, and on-chain activity to gauge community trust.
- **Historical incidents** — Has this platform been involved in any hacks, exploits, or regulatory actions?

## The Risk Score

Every analysis produces a **risk score from 0 to 100**, along with a color-coded rating:

| Score | Rating | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 0–25 | Low Risk | Well-established, audited, no known issues |
| 26–50 | Moderate | Some flags, proceed with caution |
| 51–75 | High Risk | Multiple red flags, research before using |
| 76–100 | Critical | Strong indicators of fraud or compromise |

## Detailed Annotations

Beyond the score, you get a detailed breakdown with specific annotations — each one explaining *why* a particular factor raised or lowered the risk level. This isn't a black box; you can see exactly what the AI considered.

## How to Use It

1. Open any app in CryptDocker
2. Click the **AI panel** on the right sidebar
3. Switch to the **Risk Analysis** tab
4. Hit **Analyze** — results appear in seconds

Risk Analysis is available to all **CryptDocker Pro** users. We believe security tooling should be accessible, and we're working on bringing a lighter version to the free tier in the future.
